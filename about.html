About Noccelo

Noccelo's objective

Noccelo is an AI research company. Our goal is to make artificial intelligence(AI) more accessible and widespread to ensure that it becomes beneficial and safe for humanity. We strive to use all the influence we have to prevent the AI from undermining freedom and human rights. Therefore noccelo offers optimized services to better understand the issues and the impact that artificial intelligence will have.

Noccelo also seeks to develop the entertainment sector in AI. Not only to spread it but also to improve its management. We therefore contribute to the development of creative artificial intelligence and provide its best innovations. To cope with a society that is always progressing more and more, AI is certainly the best option. That's why Noccelo facilitates access to understanding and discovery of it. Artificial intelligence will be what we make of it and we are happy to collaborate on it.
